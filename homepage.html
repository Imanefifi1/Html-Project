Popular Electronic music artists :


Zedd, born Anton Zaslavski, is a Grammy-winning DJ and music producer known for hits like "Clarity".
He blends EDM, pop, and house, collaborating with stars like Selena Gomez and Ariana Grande.
Alesso, born Alessandro Lindblad, is a Swedish DJ and music producer known for his melodic progressive house tracks.
He gained international fame with hits like "Heroes (We Could Be)" and has collaborated with artists like Calvin Harris and Tove Lo.
Calvin Harris, born Adam Richard Wiles, is a Scottish DJ, producer, and songwriter known for his chart-topping hits and collaborations with artists like Rihanna, Dua Lipa, and Ellie Goulding.
He is a leading figure in the EDM and pop music scenes.
David Guetta is a French DJ, producer, and songwriter known for his influential role in bringing electronic dance music (EDM) to mainstream audiences.
He has produced numerous global hits and collaborated with artists like Sia, Nicki Minaj, and Usher.
